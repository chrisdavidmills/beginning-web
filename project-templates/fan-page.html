Did you know?

  1. Guest vocals on Tommy the Cat were provided by Tom Waits.
  2. Primus frontman Les Claypool once auditioned to play bass in Metallica.
  3. Primus are obsessed with fishing, and have written many songs about it, such as John the Fisherman and That Ol' Diamondbacked Sturgeon
  4. At one point the only two artists on the Interscope record label were Primus and Vanilla Ice.

Biography

Primus are an alt-rock band from El Sobrante, California, who rose to fame in the early 90's with strange funky rock songs such as Jerry Was a Race Car Driver and Tommy the Cat. More recently, they are known for composing the theme tune for the TV series South Park.

More detailed information can be found at the Primus Wikipedia page.
